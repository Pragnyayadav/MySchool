Ashiana Public School

Ashiana Public School is an education institution which aims to centre for holistic and integral development epitomizing world class education for all its students. Ashiana believes in determination to work towards holistic and integral development of each student. It empowers students with the knowledge and skills to meet the complex demands of modern society as responsible individuals. At Ashiana Public School, the goal has always been to groom the children at all levels so as to provide them equal opportunity to learn and assimilate things independently.
